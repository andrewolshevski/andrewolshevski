About

As Senior Enrollment Advisor, I sold & managed custom B2B training programs. Then, as Manager of Talent & Culture, I scaled our best-in-class experience to 4 U.S. cities + online, managing a staff of 200+ instructors. Finally, as Head of Product, I built our learning platform and helped lead the company to successful acquisition in 2021.
